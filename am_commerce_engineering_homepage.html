About AM Commerce Engineering

AM Commerce Engineering is the business brand of Anna Liza Mayor, a developer focused on e-commerce systems, custom development, and technical problem solving for online stores.

The work is best suited for businesses that need practical engineering support, not just visual website setup. That includes custom functionality, troubleshooting, system improvements, and platform-specific problem solving.

How projects are handled

  1. Review the business problem, current setup, and technical constraints.

  2. Break work into manageable, testable milestones.

  3. Develop practical solutions with clear progress and feedback loops.

  4. Launch, monitor, and support improvements after delivery.

Best fit clients

Businesses already selling online and needing more advanced technical work.

Teams that need help with WooCommerce, Magento, Shopware, or custom platform integrations.

Store owners who need reliable support for rescue work, system fixes, or growth-focused improvements.
